About Me

Diving deep into the intricate world of game design, I am currently pursuing a PhD that seamlessly blends my passion and academic pursuits. My research focus is a testament to my commitment to pushing the boundaries of conventional game interfaces. I specialize in UI (User Interface) and UX (User Experience) design, ensuring that the player's journey is not only immersive but also intuitive. Beyond just design, I delve into user research, which aids me in understanding player needs and behaviors. This empirical approach is bolstered by rigorous testing, ensuring that every design decision is validated through real-world feedback. The final piece of my research puzzle is analysis; evaluating and dissecting every facet of the user experience to craft games that resonate, engage, and captivate. Join me in my quest to redefine gaming, one interface at a time.
